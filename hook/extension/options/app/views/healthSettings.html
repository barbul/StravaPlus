How to use custom Heart Rate Reserve zones ?

Edit existing zone

Click into HRR fields of the zone you want to edit. A zone is always defined between 2 HRR fields

Hint: You can use input arrows or keyboard arrows to set the value.

Add a Last HRR zone

Click “Add a Last Zone” button to add a new zone at the end of currents zones. The previous last zone will be splitted twice to add the new zone.

Remove HRR zone

Click “Del Last Zone” button to remove the zone at the end of currents zones. The 2 latest zones will be merged to perform this operation.

Reset HRR zones

Click “Reset Zones” to apply default factory zones.

What is Heart Rate Reserve ?

Heart Rate Reserve (HRR) is the difference between your Maximum Heart Rate (Max HR) and Resting Heart Rate (Rest HR). This is basically your global heart capacity :)

%HRR = 0% is your current Rest HR
%HRR = 100% is your current Max HR

What are Heart Rate Reserve Zones ?

Heart Rate Reserve Zones represents a distribution of your heart efforts during an activity into zones. Each zone has a beginning and ending HRR value.

Where to see HRR Zone distribution ?

Inside a strava cycling activity (running coming soon...). Of course you must have heart rate data associated to your activity.
